Home Loan Interest Rates & Charges

Transparent information as per bank and NBFC policies

Interest Rates
  • Interest rates are decided by the respective banks or NBFCs.
  • Rates depend on factors such as CIBIL score, income profile, loan amount, tenure, and property type.
  • Most home loans are offered at floating / repo-linked rates.
  • Final applicable rate may vary from customer to customer.

Our role is to help you identify the most suitable lender based on your profile — we do not influence bank pricing.

Charges & Fees
  • Any processing, legal, or technical fees are charged directly by the bank or NBFC, as per their internal policies.
  • Government levies such as stamp duty and registration charges are applicable as per state regulations.
  • Prepayment or foreclosure terms are governed by the lender’s policy.

Important: We do not collect any service, advisory, processing, or commission fees from clients.

Eligibility & Documentation

Simple, transparent criteria guided by Albatross home loan experts

  • Applicant Type: Salaried, self-employed professionals, and business owners
  • Age Requirement: 21 – 65 years (loan tenure aligned with retirement age)
  • Income Stability: Minimum 2–3 years of consistent income preferred
  • Credit Profile: CIBIL score of 700+ recommended for best interest rates
  • Property Type: Approved residential properties only
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar / PAN)
  • Proof of Address
  • Income Proof (Salary slips / ITR)
  • Property Documents
  • Employment / Business Details
