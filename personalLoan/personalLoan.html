Personal Loan Eligibility & Documentation

Simple, transparent criteria guided by Albatross home loan experts

  • Applicant Type: Salaried, self-employed professionals, and business owners
  • Age Requirement: 21 – 60 years (loan tenure aligned with retirement age)
  • Income Stability: Minimum 1–2 years of consistent income preferred
  • Credit Profile: CIBIL score of 700+ recommended for best interest rates
  • Loan Purpose: Personal expenses, debt consolidation, medical, education, travel, etc.
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar / PAN)
  • Proof of Address
  • Income Proof (Salary slips / ITR / Bank Statements)
  • Employment / Business Details
  • Any other supporting documents required by the lender
