Mortgage Loan Interest Rates & Charges

Clear, property-backed loan pricing as per bank & NBFC policies

Interest Rates
  • Mortgage loans are secured loans backed by residential or commercial property.
  • Interest rates generally start lower compared to unsecured loans.
  • Rates depend on property value, CIBIL score, income, LTV ratio, and tenure.
  • Most lenders offer floating / repo-linked rates.

We help you compare lenders offering the best rates based on your property profile.

Charges & Fees
  • Processing, legal, and technical valuation charges are levied by the lender.
  • Stamp duty & registration charges apply as per state laws.
  • Foreclosure and part-prepayment rules vary by lender.

No advisory or commission fees are charged by Albatross Associates.

Mortgage Loan Eligibility & Documentation

Structured, property-backed criteria guided by Albatross mortgage loan experts

  • Applicant Type: Salaried individuals, self-employed professionals, and business owners
  • Age Requirement: 21 – 65 years (loan tenure aligned with retirement age)
  • Income Stability: Minimum 2–3 years of stable income or business continuity
  • Credit Profile: CIBIL score of 650+ preferred for competitive interest rates
  • Property Type: Residential or commercial property with clear and marketable title
  • Loan Purpose: Business expansion, working capital, debt consolidation, education, or personal needs
  • Proof of Identity (Aadhaar / PAN)
  • Proof of Address
  • Income Proof (Salary slips / ITR / Bank Statements)
  • Property Documents (Sale Deed, Title Deed, Approved Plan)
  • Property Valuation & Legal Clearance (as per lender requirement)
  • Employment / Business Proof

Our advisors assist you with property verification and documentation for faster approval.
